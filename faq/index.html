General

What is Aidtree?

Aidtree is a fundraising social network platform that enables users to save money on everyday purchases while simultaneously contributing to causes they support. Businesses on our network get free advertising, generate more revenue, and build goodwill with causes by offering customizable discounts.

What are “Trees”?

Trees are categories of causes, i.e. school-affiliated organizations.

How does Aidtree work?

Businesses on the Aidtree network can offer coupons which discount their goods or services, and a portion of that discount is donated to causes when the coupon is redeemed by users.

How are coupons redeemed?

Coupons are redeemed in two ways.

1). For digital coupons: Simply apply the discount shown on the coupon and then select “Redeem”.

2). For physical coupons: Scan the QR code or enter the code on the coupon on http://aidtree.com/redeem.

Does Aidtree charge money for its service?

Aidtree charges no registration or subscription fees for using our services. We only charge a small portion of the coupon discounts at the time of redemption.

Who are we?

Aidtree was founded in 2012 by Benjamin Yip, a dedicated father and husband. As of now, he is the only full-time employee working on his dream to streamline the way that people support the causes that are important to them.

Does Aidtree charge money for its service?

Aidtree charges no registration or subscription fees for using our services. We only charge a small portion of the coupon discounts at the time of redemption.

How is this different than traditional fundraisers?

The money generated through Aidtree comes from ANY business that chooses to support your cause. This means that fundraising is not limited by time, accessibility, or location. It is continuous and users save money on daily purchases while donating.

How do I know that the organization I am supporting is legitimate?

Aidtree performs a verification process and tries its best to protect its users from fraud. However, the best way to avoid being scammed is to be aware of the causes you choose to support.

What does Aidtree do to protect its users from scamming or fraud?

If any contradictory information is found on the cause’s application, it will be flagged and examined more thoroughly. If the cause is found to be illegitimate, it will be rejected and banned from Aidtree.

How do I report illegitimate Trees?

If you find a Tree that you find to be suspicious, we encourage you to immediately send us an e-mail at QuestionableTree@aidtree.com. Be sure to include the Tree name as well as your name and your contact information, including phone number. We will not examine the Tree unless enough information is provided.

Why do I get a refund on the coupon after 30 days?

If the coupon is not used within 30 days of purchase, the amount gets refunded minus the transaction cost in order to protect the user.

What is the age requirement to use Aidtree?

You must be 14 years of age to use Aidtree.

If I have more questions, what do I do?

You can contact us at questions@aidtree.com and we'll get back to you.

Causes

How do I create a cause?

You can sign your cause up here: aidtree.com/trees/new NOTE: The opening of unauthorized accounts is grounds for legal action and potential forfeiture of using Aidtree.com.

Can I create a Tree that is not a school or non-profit organization?

Currently, Aidtree only supports these two types of Trees. Support for other Trees coming soon!

How do I get members of my cause to use Aidtree?

Once they register on the site, they can search for coupons that support your cause.

How does money flow into my cause?

Aidtree uses the financial clearinghouse Braintree for processing transactions between the causes and businesses. Braintree supports the largest websites in the world such as AirBnB and Uber. Funds from a rebate-coupon purchase are held in escrow until the coupon is used. This allows the refund (minus transaction costs) if the User decides not to use the rebate-coupon for any reason.

What is a Coupon Seed and how do I use it?

Coupon Seeds are codes given to businesses by causes.When businesses enter the code on the Aidtree website, they begin to directly support the cause whose Coupon Seed they were given.

Bussiness

How do I connect with causes?

You can send fundraising invitations to causes through the Aidtree network.

How do I alter the discounts I offer?

You can change them at any time through our site. Any coupons purchased prior to the change will not be affected.

What do I do if I want to offer individual discounts for less than $2.50?

You can use the multiple coupon option and sell bundles of smaller discounts that total $2.50.
